Flashcard improvement patch
===========================

This patch fixes the flashcard filter issue where choosing Final could appear to keep the same card because nearly every vocabulary item carried the final exam tag.

Changes:
- Rebuilt Flashcards into real study decks: All, Midterm 1 focus, Midterm 2/OOP focus, and Final focus.
- Added a topic filter inside each deck.
- Added shuffle and previous-card controls.
- Added recall prompts, study focus notes, common traps, and quality-check text.
- Expanded the vocabulary bank from 45 to 80 flashcards.
- Added tests/validate-flashcards.js so the flashcard deck cannot regress into shallow or broken filtering.

No quest modes were removed or changed.
